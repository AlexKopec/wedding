Venue / Hotel

Casonda Los Cedros is a boutique hotel in the heart of the Yucatan in the village of Espita (2.5 hours west of Cancun).You will be able to enjoy a traditional architecture through the renovation of an old colonial building but also a more contemporary architecture with the creation of eight rooms, an infinity pool and a restaurant nestled in a tropical garden.

We have booked the entire hotel for our guests to enjoy.

Wedding

We will hold the ceremony and reception at the hotel.

Reception will include a special 3 course dinner and wine pairing by Chef Jorge Ildefonso followed by local deserts and a wedding cake.

Activies

We have a few possible additional activities lined up for our time in Espita.

Brunch the day after the wedding with typical cooks: preparation of a pib (cooked underground), homemade meringues

Visit to Ek Balam archaeological site and Cenote X´canche (driver + guide + entrance)

Guided tour of the village of Espita on foot with visits to local artisans. (About 2 hours)

Performance of traditional Yucatan dances (about 45 minutes)

FAQ

When is the wedding?
  • The wedding ceremony will take place at 5pm on January 17th, 2025.
Where will the ceremony and reception be?
  • Casano Los Cedros Hotel, Espita, Yuc., Mexico
  • The ceremony, cocktail hour, dinner and reception will all be at the hotel.
Is there a dress code?
  • The dress code for our wedding is formal.
  • The ceremony will be outdoors, cocktail hour and dinner will be in an indoor/outdoor area.
Weather
  • The average high in Espita in January is 83, and the average low 63. January is typically a drier month in regards to rainfall.
What is being covered?
  • We are covering your hotel accommodations, rehearsal dinner, wedding reception drinks & dinner, brunch day after, and transportation.
  • You will need to arrange for your own flights.
Getting to Espita
  • We will provide an “ideal” arrival time soon so that all can share the bus to the hotel
  • Wednesday January 15th at a time TBD in the early afternoon for the few that will arrive on Wednesday.
  • Thursday January 16th at a time TBD in the early afternoon for most guests.
  • We will also provide a shuttle for departures on Sunday, January 19th. Check out from the hotel and airbnb is at 11am and transportation to the airport will be soon after.
Accommodations
  • We booked the entire hotel Casona Los Cedros (8 rooms) for our guests. We have also booked Casa Moca, an airbnb just a couple minutes walk from the hotel for additional guests.
  • Room assignments will be in your invitations!
Menu/Food
  • The hotel Chef is working on preparing a menu for dinner after the ceremony - stay tuned!
  • There is a restaurant and bar at the hotel that will be open for you to enjoy for other meals.
  • We would love for everyone to join us for a welcome/rehearsal dinner at the hotel on Thursday, Jan. 18 if you are able.
Activities
  • We are planning an excursion day on Saturday the 18th to visit Chichen Itza - one of the “New 7 Wonders of the World” and visit a local cenote along with lunch for anyone who would like to join us.
